## F.A.Q's

<ProjectCard
  title="What is a contruction clean?"
  link="https://builder.bookipi.com/pages/build-clean"
  bg="linear-gradient(to right, #D4145A 0%, #FBB03B 100%)"
>
  Construction cleaning refers to the process of cleaning up a construction site after the completion of a project. It involves removing debris, dust, and other construction-related waste to make the site clean and ready for occupancy or further construction activities.
</ProjectCard>
<ProjectCard
  title="Why is construction cleaning important?"
  link="https://builder.bookipi.com/pages/build-clean"
  bg="linear-gradient(to right, #662D8C 0%, #ED1E79 100%)"
>
  Construction cleaning is important for several reasons. Firstly, it ensures the safety of workers and future occupants by removing any hazards or potential risks from the site. Secondly, a clean construction site demonstrates professionalism and attention to detail, which can leave a positive impression on clients and stakeholders. Lastly, proper cleaning helps to preserve the quality and longevity of the construction materials and finishes.
</ProjectCard>

  title="Do construction cleaning services provide equipment and supplies?"
